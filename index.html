Facts about the chocolate:

In the 1930's, the chocolates were known as "penny cups" due to only costing a penny per chocolate.

The peanut butter cups actually have about as much protein in them as a handful of almonds.

A history of Reese's Peanut Butter Cups:

Reese's peanut butter cups were first created in 1928. They were created by a man named H.B. Reese who was a former dairy farmer. Reese used to work with Hershey, and was inspired by him. He left dairy farming behind to start his own candy business.
